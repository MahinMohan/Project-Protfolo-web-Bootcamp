My Top 3 Movies of all Time

1. Ford Vs Ferrari

This is my favourite movie as it potrays the amazing rivalry between two legendary companies Ferrari and Ford
The two companies battle it out at Le-Mans an iconic race track bringing their respective supercars to win the title.

2. The art of Racing in the Rain

Being a car enthusiast, this is another one of my top rated movies.
The movie depicts the life of a professinal racer and the personal life hardships he encounters throughout his racing career.

3. Interstellar

Lastly this Christopher Nolan stellar movie is absolutely marvelous.
For all the sci-fi lovers this is a must watch as it potrays all the scientific aspects of space and beyond.
